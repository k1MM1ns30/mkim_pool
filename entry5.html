PARIS

MGallery Molitor

POOL TYPE: #BSF5L48

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the curved ceiling.

Facility

Molitor Pool was constructed in Paris in 1929 and remains as a historic swimming facility. The pool is known for its vivid architectural colors, especially the bright yellow and blue facade. This color scheme reflects the Art Deco architectural style of its time.

Informaiton

Location : 16 Av. de la Prte Molitor, 75016 Paris, France

Operating hours : 7 a.m. – 10 p.m. ( for hotel guests and members only )

Admission : Free for hotel guests.

Non-guests can book a ‘Lunch & Swim’ offer for €230, or a day pass (€290 for two people)
