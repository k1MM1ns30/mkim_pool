Santamonica Swim Center

POOL TYPE: #ODF13L25

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the continuous curved geometry applied throughout the roof, walls, and diving platforms.

Facility

London Aquatics Centre was constructed for the 2012 London Olympics and designed by architect Zaha Hadid. The architectural form reflects the fluid geometry of water, expressed through its continuous curved roof structure. As an Olympic facility, the building remains well preserved. Light enters the interior through the blue-tinted facade, creating a distinct spatial and visual condition.
