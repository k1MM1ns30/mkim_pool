SYDNEY

Bondi Icebergs Pool

POOL TYPE: #ODS8L50

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the curved ceiling.

Facility

Bondi Icebergs Pool is located directly beside the ocean at the edge of Bondi Beach. Originally constructed in 1929, it is filled with seawater, allowing waves to continuously flow into the pool. The pool is well known for its unique location beside the sea. Due to the constant wave movement and its relatively deep depth, caution is required for inexperienced swimmers.

Informaiton

Location : 1 Notts Ave, Bondi Beach NSW 2026, Australia

Operating hours : 6 a.m. – 6:30 p.m. ( Closed on Thu )

Admission : Adult $10 ( per 1pass )
