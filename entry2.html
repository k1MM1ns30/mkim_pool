SEOUL

Seoul Olympic
Swimming Pool

POOL TYPE: #IDF10L50

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the curved ceiling.

Facility

Olympic Swimming Pool was the venue for the swimming events during the 1988 Seoul Olympics. It features a main pool with an international-standard 50-meter length. In addition to the main pool, the facility includes a secondary 25-meter pool and a diving pool with a depth of 5 meters. As a structure built in an earlier period, the building retains a retro architectural character.

Informaiton

Location : 424 Olympic-ro, Songpa District, Seoul, South Korea

Operating hours : 6 a.m. – 10 p.m.

Admission : Adult ₩7,000 (50min)

Child ₩6,000

*Entry is restricted to children under 140 cm or born in 2019 or later for safety reasons.*
