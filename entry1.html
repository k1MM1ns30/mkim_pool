TOKYO

Aqua Field Shiba Park

POOL TYPE: #OSF8L50

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the tensile canopy at the entrance.

Facility

Aqua Field Shiba Park Swimming Pool is known for its view of Tokyo Tower from the pool.
The pool was renovated with a movable floor system, replacing the original concrete bottom. During the summer, it functions as a swimming pool, but in other seasons, the floor can be raised to the same level as the poolside. When the floor is raised, the space is used as a futsal court instead of a pool.

Informaiton

Location : 2 Chome-7-2 Shibakoen, Minato City, Tokyo 105-0011, Japan

Operating hours : 9 a.m. – 8 p.m. ( Open only from July 1 to September 15 )

Admission : Adult ¥500 (2h) / ¥250 (extra hour)

Child ¥200 (2h) / ¥100 (extra hour)

Free — Infants, 65+, disabilities

*Adult supervision required Max 2 children per adult*
