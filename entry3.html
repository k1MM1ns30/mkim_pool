HONG KONG

Kennedy Town
Swimming Pool

POOL TYPE: #OSF9L50

Architect

The key architectural element of this pool is the curved roof.

Facility

Kennedy Town Swimming Pool is located in Hong Kong and is surrounded by high-rise buildings. This creates a different spatial feeling compared to swimming pools in more open areas. The building was designed based on the surrounding railway and residential environment. It has a curved roof, while the outdoor pool remains open, creating a continuous connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.

Informaiton

Location : Hong Kong, Kennedy Town, Sai Cheung St, 北2號

Operating hours : 6:30 a.m. – 10 p.m.

Admission : Adult weekdays $17, weekends $20

Concessionary Weekdays $8, Weekends $9

*Entry is restricted to children under 140 cm or born in 2019 or later for safety reasons.*
